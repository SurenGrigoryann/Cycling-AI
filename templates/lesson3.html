Paper That Can NOT Be Recycled
Some paper is coated, waxed, or plastic-lined — these types ruin entire batches of paper recycling.
❌ Goes in Garbage
🧾 Thermal receipts ☕ Paper cups 🪞 Waxed paper 🏷️ Stickers 🗂️ Laminated paper
Why?
These have plastic or wax coatings that don't separate during recycling and contaminate the paper pulp.

Always Flatten Cardboard
Large boxes take up enormous space in recycling bins and trucks. Flattening them means more recycling fits — and costs less to collect.
1
Remove any packing material inside (bubble wrap, styrofoam → Garbage)
2
Flatten the box completely
3
Put the flat cardboard in the recycling bin

Paper Can Be Recycled 7 Times
Paper fibres get shorter every time they're recycled. After about 7 cycles, they're too short to use — but by then, your paper has done enormous good!
17
trees saved by recycling one tonne of paper. Plus 7,000 gallons of water and enough energy to power a home for 6 months.
