Why You Must Rinse First

Plastics That Can NOT Be Recycled

What About Caps and Lids?
A lot of people remove caps before recycling. But that's actually wrong — here's the correct rule:
💡 The cap rule
Leave caps ON the bottle when recycling. Loose caps are too small — they fall through sorting machinery and end up in landfill anyway.
Paper labels are fine to leave on too — the recycling process handles them automatically.
